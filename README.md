## 📌 Current Research Direction

### PhD Research Theme

**Trustworthy AI for Smart Energy Systems: Explainable, Reliable, and Uncertainty-Aware Forecasting and Decision Support**

### Research Focus

This research aims to develop trustworthy AI methodologies for smart energy systems by integrating calibrated uncertainty quantification, reliability under real-world distribution shifts, and explainability for operator-centric decision support. The research begins with uncertainty-aware load forecasting as a foundational use case, then investigates model robustness under evolving grid conditions, extreme weather events, and sensor anomalies, before developing explainability mechanisms that enhance transparency and trust in operational decision-making. Collectively, these contributions aim to support trustworthy, real-time AI decision support for safety-critical energy applications.
